Solar Water Heating

Solar can save up to 80% of water heating costs.

Water heating systems are often the second biggest user of electricity, about 30% of total electricity costs. Savings vary according to water usage, family size, age and type of a structure, tank size, and water temperature preferences. Our solar tanks are thermally insulated for maximum heat storage, minimum heat loss, and provide back up electrical element heat during extremely heavy usage.
