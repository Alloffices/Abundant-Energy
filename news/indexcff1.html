Lemonade!

Posted on December 26, 2015 by Bill Hoysradt

When our previous website was hacked and vicious things were done to it, we saw an opportunity to make lemonade from the lemons. We saw a crossroads, and the path we chose was to expand our communications with our clients and the South Florida community interested in solar energy.

Our new website is the best Christmas present we could imagine; thanks for all of you who recommended us to Santa’s ‘nice’ list! We’d like to keep you informed on important issues affecting energy conservation in Florida, as well as local items of interest in South Florida and the Treasure Coast.

As the New Year approaches, we thank you for the projects that you have given us, and invite you to subscribe to our newsletter.

Please contact us at any time.
— Bill
